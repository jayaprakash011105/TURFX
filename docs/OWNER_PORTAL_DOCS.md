# 🏢 TURFX: VENUE MANAGER PORTAL (VMP) 🏏🏟️✨

Welcome to the **Owner Experience Suite**—the platform's high-fidelity mission control for sports venue owners. This portal provides real-time oversight and reputation management for peak venue performance.

---

### 🚀 MISSION OBJECTIVES:
*   **📊 Dynamic Oversight:** Real-time analytics for revenue, bookings, and customer trends across your entire venue portfolio. 💹💹📉
*   **📡 Bi-Directional Harmony:** Every user action (booking, review, payment) is instantly synced with your Manager Dashboard. 📡🛰️✅
*   **🛡️ Reputation Moderation:** A dedicated Feedback Hub for replying to customers, marking helpful insights, and archiving feedback. 🗣️👑🛡️
*   **🎟️ Dynamic Promotions:** Launch, edit, and track marketing strategies (Percentage % or Flat ₹) that update instantly in the player app. 📈📉🎯

---

### 🛰️ KEY ENGINE MODULES:

#### 📂 1. VENUE PORTFOLIO (`VenuesPage.jsx`)
*   **Court Intelligence:** View and manage individual court availability for all your properties. 🏟️📂✨
*   **Status Indicators:** Real-time visual feedback on "Open", "Blocked", or "Booked" slots. 📅💹✅

#### 🛡️ 2. REPUTATION HUB (`ReviewsPage.jsx`)
*   **Official Responses:** Publish platform-certified merchant replies to build customer trust. 🗣️✍️👑
*   **Moderation Engine:** Archive or delete reviews to maintain a premium venue reputation. 🚫📂✅
*   **Global Merit Score:** Real-time average rating calculation based on verified customer feedback. ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

#### 🎟️ 3. PROMOTION ENGINE (`PromotionsPage.jsx`)
*   **Campaign Launchpad:** Deploy "Welcome" or "Winter" discount codes with precision. 🚀🎟️✨
*   **Usage Constraints:** Set usage limits and expiration dates to protect your venue's ROI. 📊💹📉
*   **Real-Time Sync:** Modified coupons are instantly updated for users in the Discovery Portal. 🛰️💹✅

---

### 🛠️ ARCHITECTURAL STACK:
1.  **Store:** Driven by a centralized, reactive `AppContext` for 100% data parity across all portals. 📂🧬🛰️
2.  **UX:** Built with high-fidelity React components and **Lucide-React** for premium visual status reporting. 🎨✨✅
3.  **Real-Time Flow:** Employs a zero-latencey state push-architecture, ensuring a "Single Source of Truth." 🏎️📊💹
